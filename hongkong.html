Hong Kong

Why I love Hong Kong

Hong Kong is such an incredible place to visit, and there are so many things I love about it. First of all, the city’s skyline is breathtaking—those towering skyscrapers set against Victoria Harbour are just stunning, especially at night when the lights reflect off the water. It feels like you’re stepping into the future. But Hong Kong isn’t just about modern architecture; it’s also rich in culture and history. I love wandering around the bustling streets of Mong Kok, trying all the amazing street food—egg waffles, dim sum, roast goose—you name it, it’s delicious. Plus, Hong Kong is a shopper’s paradise. From the luxury shops in Central to the quirky street markets, there’s something for everyone. And then there’s Disneyland, which is just pure fun if you’re in the mood for something whimsical. But what I think I love most about Hong Kong is how it perfectly blends city life with nature. Hiking trails like Dragon’s Back are just a short ride away from the busy city, giving you amazing views of both the lush mountains and the sparkling sea.

How to get there?

Getting to Hong Kong from the Philippines is pretty straightforward. All you really have to do is book a direct flight from Manila to Hong Kong International Airport, which only takes around two hours. It's super convenient and flights are frequent, so you can pretty much choose whichever time suits you best. Once you land, you can take the Airport Express or a taxi to the city center. It’s such an easy trip that it makes Hong Kong one of the most accessible international destinations for a quick getaway.
