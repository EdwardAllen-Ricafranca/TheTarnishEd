Ireland

Why I love Ireland

Ireland has always fascinated me with its lush, rolling landscapes, charming towns, and rich history. The Cliffs of Moher are a must-see, with their dramatic drop into the Atlantic and breathtaking views. I also love the idea of exploring Dublin’s vibrant culture, from traditional pubs with live music to historical sites like Trinity College and the Book of Kells. The warmth of the locals is famous, and I imagine myself enjoying friendly chats and soaking up the cozy atmosphere. Plus, the castles and ancient ruins scattered across the countryside feel like something straight out of a fairytale. I can’t wait to experience that perfect mix of natural beauty, history, and culture.

How to get there?

To get to Ireland from the Philippines, I’d have to catch a flight with at least one or two layovers, usually stopping in major hubs like Dubai, Doha, or London before heading to Dublin or Shannon Airport.
